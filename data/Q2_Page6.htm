May 21, 2016 | Stage 7

Santa Rosa will host Stage 7 of the the Amgen Tour of California on May 21, 2016! #KINGSTAGE

Latest Press Release: Amgen Tour of California Launches its Second Decade as America’s Premier Cycling Race with Electrifying International Field

Known as one of the top cycling destinations in the world, Santa Rosa hosted the first ever Stage 1 of the Amgen Tour of California, is home to three-time Amgen Tour of California winner Levi Leipheimer and his renowned Levi’s GranFondo, home of the US headquarters of the 2011 Tour de France winning Team BMC, and host to training camps for many elite pro-cycling teams, as well as amateur clubs and bike-passionate individuals.
