Tour of California

America’s Grand Tour

We are now taking reservations for the 2017 Tour of California. These trips sell out every year so give us a call at 866 464 8735866 464 8735 today!

The Amgen Tour of California is America’s biggest stage race. Join us for an amazing adventure with epic riding, stunning views, and exciting live race action, and you’ll see why. This trip, set amidst some of California's most spectacular landscapes, takes you to the heart of the action to enjoy thrilling race stages with all the comfort and benefits of unrivaled VIP team access. Ride like a pro up the challenging climbs of Angeles National Forest, with cheering crowds with an amazing support of Trek Travel guides. Witness the dramatic mountain finish on the famous Gibraltar road while sipping your cocktails in an exclusive Amgen hospitality tent. Cap it all off with amazing behind-the-scenes access to the Trek-Segafredo Team. This trip is the ultimate celebration of American pro racing, fantastic riding and the awesome natural beauty of California!

Day

1

Today's Ride:

Tuna Canyon Loop
Approximately 34 miles with 2,726 feet of climbing

Today's Activity:

Team Access Night

Description:

Your Trek Travel guides will meet you at the hotel The Langham Huntington at 11:00 AM on the first day of the trip. Your Tour of California adventure begins as your guides introduce you to the features of your Trek bike and ensure a perfect, comfortable fit, before discussing the rules of bike safety and offering some specific cycling tips for riding in California. Then it’s off for a warm-up ride! Shake off your travel legs riding around La Tuna Canyo, with a lap around the famous Rose Bowl stadium, followed by lunch along the way with your companions. Tonight, enjoy an exclusive “behind-the-scenes” meet-and-greet over drinks with representatives from the Trek-Segafredo team. You’ll sip cocktails and get an insider perspective on team strategy for a pro race evening you’ll never forget!

Day

2

Today's Ride:

South Pasadena to Santa Clarita
Approximately 52 miles with 6,053 feet of climbing

Today's Activity:

Riding on the Stage 2 course.

Description:

You may want to pin on a number today, because this day is all about the race! Feel the excitement of the sign-in and staging in South Pasadena. Much of today's stage contests in the Angeles National Forest, ending with the thrilling conclusion in veteran host city Santa Clarita. You’ll have the chance to experience the on-course support, encouragement and expertise of your Trek Travel guides as you ride amazing Trek bikes around the breathtaking beauty of the San Gabriel Mountains. Feel the electricity surge through the crowd as the racers draw near and cheer your favorite riders on their way to the finish line. After the stage finish we will shuttle 1.5 hours to our next hotel in Santa Barbara for a gourmet dinner at the hotel's restaurant. Tonight, rest your legs for tomorrow's finish on the mighty Gibraltar Road.

Day

3

Today's Ride:

Santa Barbara to Carpinteria to East Mountain Drive Loop with Gibraltar Road
Approximately 51 miles with 6,100 feet of climbing

Today's Activity:

Live VIP Viewing at Stage 3 Finish

Description:

Prepare for the beauty of the “American Riviera” and a day of live race excitement. Saddle up to ride on the race course with a thrilling, first-time mountaintop finish at Gibraltar Road. The jaw-dropping scenery makes this one of the prettiest rides in North America, with stunning panoramic views of private estates in the hills above and the glittering blue expanse of ocean below. Once you crest the summit of today’s challenge you’ll be rewarded with an up-close and personal viewing spot in the Amgen VIP hospitality tent where you’ll witness the thrilling finish! Enjoy refreshing drinks and snacks as you watch the approaching peloton on big screen TVs featuring live race coverage. You won’t miss a second of the action between the riders rolling by and the GC-defining finish. Tonight, discover local favorite bistros for a dinner on your own.

4

Today's Ride:

Santa Barbara to Casitas Pass Summit Loop
Approximately 41 miles with 2,700 feet of climbing

Description:

After a hearty breakfast, you will enjoy a memorable ride from the hotel to Carpinteria and up into the ascending and descending roads of the scenic Gobenador Canyon. For those looking for a bit more climbing, continue up to Casitas Pass Summit where the racers battled in last year's of Tour of California. After you take in a view of the Ojai Valley, wind your way back to the coast. This afternoon you will have time to explore the variety of shops and galleries of downtown Santa Barbara or hunt for a few last unique local delicacies to bring home. Tonight, raise your glass with your fellow travelers to this year’s victorious champion over a farewell feast of fresh, local California cuisine.

FAQs

Trip Changes

Our itineraries are an approximation of our trips. Trek Travel strives to offer each and every guest a vacation of a lifetime on every trip. At times routes, hotels, activities, etc. listed here may change at the discretion of the trip designer or guides to improve the trip experience.

What are the daily rides like?
  • Rider Type | 3
  • Average Daily Mileage | 40
  • Terrain | Hilly
  • Average Daily Elevation | 3,700 ft

On all of our vacations, Trek Travel strives to find quiet, paved secondary roads or bike paths to experience a region; busy roads are avoided as much as possible. Tour of California is best suited for our Type 3 Riders. Our Trek Travel guides can also easily accommodate Type 2 Riders, who seek less mileage or fewer uphills, with a boost in the van. Type 4 Riders will enjoy the extra mileage the avid route options offer. The terrain is widely varied, from flat, ocean-side riding to steep, sustained climbing. The routes might be subject to change as more course information is released.

What are the options for a non-rider travel companion?

On most Trek Travel trips, we offer non-riding options for riders who want to take the afternoon off, or travel companions who want to spend little or no time in the saddle. Some of these options may need to be scheduled before your trip–please contact one of our Trip Consultants for more information. Expenses and/or related transportation to these activities may not be included in the trip price. See Included in the Trip Price for more information. Some non-riding options on this trip are:

  • Trek-Segafredo Team Access
  • VIP Live Race Viewing in Amgen Hospitality Area
  • Shopping
What is the weather going to be like?

You can expect to experience a wide range of temperatures and conditions on your Amgen Tour of California trip. In both areas, Angeles National Forest area and Santa Barbara County, expect temps between 50 and 80 degrees. In other words, be prepared for anything, including rain! We recommend packing layers and also checking the forecast the week before your trip in order to pack appropriately.

Do you have upgrade options available?

For an additional fee you may request to upgrade to the Trek Emonda SLR with Shimano Di2 Electronic shifting or Bontrager Aeolus carbon wheels for the ultimate riding experience. Contact our trip consultants for more details.

WHAT IF I HAVE TO CHANGE OR CANCEL MY TRIP?

We understand things happen in life and sometimes you have to cancel or change your bike tour. We want to make it as easy as possible to do so and below you will find our timeline. If you ever have a question, just give us a call, 866 464 8735866 464 8735. Read our cancellation policy.

Meeting & Departing

How to get there

We suggest that you arrive at least one day prior to the trip start, which will enable you to adjust to the time zone and minimize the risk of missing the trip start due to flight-related delays. We recommend that you fly into Burbank Bob Hope Airport and take a 15 minute ride to the hotel. A cab from the airport to Pasadena will cost approximately $40.

At the conclusion of your trip, we suggest flying home from the Santa Barbara Municipal Airport (SBA), in Goleta, approximately 8 miles from downtown Santa Barbara. Most major U.S.-based airlines serve this airport. Trek Travel will provide a shuttle to the airport as described below. If you wish to spend some additional time in Fess Parker you may depart at your leisure and arrange for your own transportation to the airport or to your next destination.

Meeting Time And Location

Langham Huntington | Pasadena, California

Your Trek Travel guides will meet you at the hotel The Langham Huntington, Pasadena, CA at 11:00 AM on the first day of the trip. Your Tour of California adventure begins as your guides introduce you to the features of your Trek bike and ensure a perfect, comfortable fit, before discussing the rules of bike safety and offering some specific cycling tips for riding in California. Then it’s off for a warm-up ride!

If you will be late for the meet-up or are going to miss it altogether, please inform your guides. You will receive an email a week before the trip start with their names and contact details. If you cannot reach them, please call our first hotel, The Langham Huntington (626 568 3900626 568 3900), and leave a message with your expected arrival time and contact details.

Departing Time and Location

Fess Parker's DoubleTree Resort | Santa Barbara, California

You will say farewell to your guides at 12:00 PM at the Fess Parker’s DoubleTree Resort. You’ll then shuttle approximately 20 minutes to Santa Barbara Municipal Airport (SBA) to arrive at approximately 12:30 PM. Please allow adequate connection time for a flight or train to accommodate possible traffic delays.
