Event Description

RIDE EVERY STAGE, JUST BEFORE THE PROS!

UPDATE: Our 2016 Amgen Tour of California Race Experience is Sold Out!

Go pro for the 2016 Amgen Tour of California! Ride every stage (May 2016), just hours before the pros. And you better be fit because this is a race – if our team is too slow we’ll be pulled off the course! Live like a pro on and off the bike with comprehensive support from soigneurs and mechanics. You’ll even eat with the pro teams and stay in the same hotels. And just like the pros, you’ll live out of one bag and face fines (donated to charity) for being late to the van, etc.

This is a once-in-a-lifetime cycling experience with CTS. This is one of the most popular Bucket List Events and it sold out in 2015, so get “Bucket List Qualified” today and register for the ATOC Race Experience before it’s full!

Includes

  • Ride every stage of the 2016 ATOC (May 2016) with CTS Coaches
  • VIP Access to all ATOC Stage Finishes
  • Professional soigneurs
  • Daily post-ride massage
  • Professional mechanics
  • Bikes cleaned and tuned after each stage
  • Complete sports nutrition support including Bonk Breaker nutrition
  • All rides are fully supported
  • 3 CTS Jerseys
  • All lodging and ground transportation during ATOC (Arrival and Departure dates TBD)
  • All meals included

CTS Insight:

This is cycling's version of the baseball fantasy camp. Spend the day riding some of the most spectacular routes in the world, cheered on by race fans and supported like a pro. And in the evenings - after your massage - have dinner with the top pros in cycling. You have to be fit for this trip, and riders have ranged from 30-68 years old. You don't have to be a racer, but you will gain an understanding of what it's like to work as a team.
